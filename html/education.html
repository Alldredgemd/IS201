Education

Here is what I've learned

Throughout the course of my education, I have learned a great deal about statistical analysis, technical ability, and communication effectiveness, among other subjects. The most important thing I have learned, however, is that all of the tools I have been taught are just that: tools.

Statistics represents a tool that helps us to make sense of the world around us, in the same way that any discipline has the same objective but uses different means to get there. When applied to a business, statistics is a tool that aids in the decision making process.

Statistics and data analysis are powerful tools, but it is unwise to rely solely on numbers and data to make critical decisions. Rather, statistical inference is one of many resources that, when fully utilized, can aid any business entity in reaching their objectives and maintaining their organizational values.
